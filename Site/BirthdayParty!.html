"Get Busy" by Sean Paul was released on January 27th 2003, as a part of the Dutty Rock album, with music produced by Steven "Lenky" Marsden. This song has gone platinum seven times across six countries (twice in canada.) The music video was directed by Julien Christian Lutz otherwise known by his industry name, Director X.

Dutty Rock was Sean Paul's second studio album. The album went triple platinum in Canada, triple platinum in the United Kingdom, triple platinum in the united states, an double platinum in france. Critical reception was middling, many review websites like AllMusic and Blender scoring it a 4/5, but others like RollingStones would score it a 2/5.
